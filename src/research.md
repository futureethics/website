## Breakthrough Research Areas

  <div class="highlight-box">
    <strong>Key Innovation:</strong> Developed the APAC Red Teaming Framework, now used by major AI labs to evaluate cross-cultural safety alignment.
  </div>

    <li><strong>Featured Achievement:</strong> Led 350+ expert red teamers across 12 countries</li>
    <li><strong>Impact:</strong> Identified 3x higher AI failure rates in non-English contexts</li>

</div>

<div class="research-area">
  <h3>Cross-Language Vulnerability Discovery</h3>
  <p class="project-subtitle">Exposing Hidden Attack Vectors</p>
  
  <p>Our research systematically identifies how AI safety measures degrade across language boundaries—revealing attack vectors invisible to English-only testing.</p>

  <h4>Breakthrough Findings:</h4>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Unicode Exploitation:</strong> Demonstrated homoglyph attacks using CJK characters bypass safety filters in 87% of tested models</li>
    <li><strong>Cultural Code-Switching:</strong> Proved cultural references and code-switching can circumvent content moderation in 12 languages</li>
    <li><strong>Script Boundary Attacks:</strong> Identified tokenization vulnerabilities across non-Latin writing systems</li>
  </ul>

  <div class="highlight-box">
    <strong>Real-World Impact:</strong> These vulnerabilities have been patched in major commercial AI systems, protecting millions of users globally.
  </div>
</div>
